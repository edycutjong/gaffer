Engine disclosure

Gaffer always labels its inference engine. qvac = the real on-device model via @qvac/sdk (LLAMA 3.2 1B GGUF; Piper voice for TTS). sim = a disclosed deterministic grammar used for development and for machines without the model — it is never presented as a language model, and TTS is disabled rather than faked. The P2P layer underneath (discovery, encrypted delegation, failover) is identical and real in both.
